Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my appointment?
We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and time. For weekend appointments or during holiday seasons, we suggest booking 3-4 weeks ahead.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.
Do you use cruelty-free products?
Yes! We are committed to using only cruelty-free, vegan-friendly products. All our brands are carefully selected for their ethical standards and high performance.
Can I bring my own products?
For hygiene and safety reasons, we generally do not allow clients to bring their own products. Our professionals use products specifically formulated for professional use and tailored to your skin type and needs.
Do you offer packages or memberships?
Yes, we offer several membership packages that provide discounts on services and products. We also create custom packages for special occasions like weddings or events. Contact us for more information.
